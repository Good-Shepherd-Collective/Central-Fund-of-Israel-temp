Donate by Check – Choose Your Country

Check donations are tax-deductible in the U.S., Israel, Canada, Australia, the U.K., France, and many European Union countries when donations are made in the relevant currencies. After your donation is processed, you will receive a thank you note and a tax receipt from Shurat HaDin or from our respective partner organization.

Donations can also be made via wire and stock transfer.
Please contact Leah at leah@israellawcenter.org for instructions and for any questions regarding donations.

USA:   

For Personal Checks:
Please make checks payable to “Central Fund of Israel” earmarked to “SHD834” and mail to:
The Central Fund of Israel
P.O. Box 491
Woodmere, NY 11598

For US Charitable Funds / Charitable Funds / IRA Donations:
Please make checks payable to “Central Fund of Israel” earmarked to “SHD834” (CFI Tax ID# 13-2992985), and mail to:
The Central Fund of Israel
P.O. Box 491
Woodmere, NY 11598
