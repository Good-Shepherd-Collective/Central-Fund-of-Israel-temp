    Policy & Guidelines

    We are a private organization.

    We do not advertise or engage in promotional campaigns.

    We never share donor information. 

    We ensure that 100% of your donation reaches approved charitable causes in Israel. ​

    We accept donations recommended for specific nonprofit organizations in Israel that comply with U.S. charitable law, align with our mission, and have been reviewed and approved by the Central Fund of Israel. To begin the approval process download our questionnaire here.
